Privacy Policy

Effective July 2026

Plain-English summary: we collect what we need to provide your care and run the service, we protect it, we don't sell it, and we never contact your employer.

What we collect

  • Health information you provide in your assessment and ongoing care — history, medications, measurements, goals.
  • Account and contact details — name, email, phone, shipping address, state of residence.
  • Payment information, processed by our payment provider; we don't store full card numbers.
  • Usage data — how you use our site and messages, to improve the service.

How we use it

  • To provide your care: provider review, prescriptions, pharmacy fulfillment, shipping, and check-ins.
  • To operate the business: billing, support, service improvement, and legal compliance.
  • To contact you about your care and, with your consent, our journal and updates — unsubscribe anytime.

What we never do

  • We never sell your personal information.
  • We never contact your employer or department.
  • We never disclose your health information except as described in our HIPAA Notice below or as required by law.

Who we share it with

Only the partners required to deliver your care — our contracted provider network, licensed pharmacies, shipping carriers (plain box, no contents on the label), payment processors, and the technology platform that hosts our clinical operations. Each is bound by contract to protect your information.

Your choices

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information (subject to medical-record retention laws) by contacting support@resewellness.com. State-specific privacy rights (including under Arizona and California law) are honored where they apply.

Terms of Service

Effective July 2026

Plain-English summary: Rese connects you with licensed providers who decide what's medically appropriate. Prices are stated before you pay. Weight programs ask a three-month start, then month-to-month. Be honest in your assessment — your safety depends on it.

The service

Rese Wellness, LLC ("Rese") operates a wellness platform that connects you with independent licensed healthcare providers and licensed pharmacies. Rese does not itself practice medicine; all clinical decisions — including whether to prescribe — are made by licensed providers in your state using their independent judgment.

Eligibility and honesty

You must be 18 or older and located in a state where our provider network operates. You agree to provide accurate, complete health information; inaccurate answers can make treatment unsafe, and providing false information voids our responsibility for outcomes it causes.

Prescriptions are never guaranteed

Payment for a program does not purchase a prescription. If a provider determines treatment isn't appropriate for you, you receive a full refund (see Refund Policy).

Pricing and billing

Every price is stated before you pay. Weight programs are billed monthly with a three-month initial commitment, then continue month-to-month; cancel future months in one step from your account or by contacting us. Prices never rise with your dose within a program.

Not for emergencies

Our service is not for medical emergencies. If you're experiencing an emergency, call 911.

The usual terms

Content on this site is educational, not medical advice. Accounts may be closed for misuse. Disputes are governed by Arizona law. We may update these terms; material changes will be notified.

Telehealth Consent

Effective July 2026

Plain-English summary: your care happens online, which has benefits and limits. A licensed provider in your state reviews your case; sometimes they'll require a video visit or labs, and you can ask for a human conversation at any point.

By using Rese you consent to receive care via telehealth: your health information is reviewed electronically by a licensed provider in your state, who may approve treatment, request more information, order labs, require a video visit where state law or clinical judgment calls for one, or decline treatment.

  • Benefits: access without travel or waiting rooms, documented communication, care that fits your schedule.
  • Limits: no physical exam; care depends on the accuracy of what you share. Your provider will tell you if your situation needs in-person care.
  • Your rights: you may withdraw this consent, request your records, or ask to speak with a person at any time — it will never delay urgent guidance.

HIPAA Notice

Effective July 2026

Plain-English summary: your health information is protected by federal law. We use it for treatment, payment, and operations — and beyond that, only with your written authorization or where the law requires.

How your health information may be used

  • Treatment — sharing with the providers and pharmacies involved in your care.
  • Payment — processing billing for your program.
  • Operations — quality improvement, safety monitoring, and compliance.
  • As required by law — court orders, public-health reporting, and similar legal duties.

Your rights

  • Get a copy of your health records, and request corrections.
  • Request restrictions on certain uses, and confidential communications (e.g., a specific phone or email).
  • Receive a list of certain disclosures, and a paper copy of this notice.
  • File a complaint — with us at support@resewellness.com or with the U.S. Department of Health & Human Services — without retaliation.

Uses beyond the above — including any marketing use of your health information — require your written authorization, which you may revoke at any time.

Cancellation Policy

Effective July 2026

Plain-English summary: declined by the provider = full refund, automatically. Prescription medication that has shipped can't be returned — federal law — but if we got something wrong, we'll make it right.

  • Provider declines you: 100% refund of anything you've paid, processed automatically — you don't have to ask.
  • You cancel before provider review: full refund.
  • Cancelling your program: cancel future months in one step from your account or by contacting us. Weight programs carry a three-month initial commitment, billed monthly; after that, month-to-month.
  • After medication ships: prescription medication cannot be returned or refunded once dispensed, per pharmacy law.
  • Our error — wrong item, damaged in transit, unusable on arrival: replaced or refunded, your choice, at no cost.

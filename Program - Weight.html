Fair questions

Asked and answered

Why is the price flat when other programs charge by dose?

Because dose-based pricing punishes you for the medicine working as designed. Titration upward is normal and expected — your price is $185/mo for semaglutide or $299/mo for tirzepatide, at every dose, period.

What's actually included?

Your medication and supplies, provider review and ongoing dose management, the week-eight Plateau Protocol, check-ins with real people, and shipping in discreet packaging. The program price is the whole price.

Why a three-month start?

It gives your provider time to start treatment safely and evaluate your response — and we'd rather tell you here than in checkout fine print. You're billed monthly. After three months it's month-to-month — cancel in one step.

What about side effects?

The most common is mild nausea around dose increases — usually temporary, and manageable with the protein-first, hydration-forward guidance your provider gives you. Your program includes side-effect support: message your clinical team any time, and your provider adjusts your titration if needed. Serious side effects are rare; your provider screens your health history for them before ever prescribing.

Which medication is right for me?

That's your provider's call, made from your health history. You can state a preference in your assessment, and your provider will tell you honestly if the other one fits better — or if neither is safe for you. Not approved = never charged.

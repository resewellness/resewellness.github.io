Fair questions

Asked and answered

Is this cheating?

No. Needing insulin isn't cheating at diabetes, and quieting food noise isn't cheating at health. Your biology changed; your tools are allowed to change too.

Who actually prescribes it?

A licensed provider in your state reviews your full health history and makes the call — a real clinician, not an algorithm. In most states there's no appointment; a few require a quick video visit, and if yours does, we'll make it painless.

What's the catch?

The closest thing to one: we ask for a three-month commitment when you start, because that's how long the medicine takes to work — and we'd rather tell you here than in checkout fine print. You're still billed monthly. After three months: month-to-month, cancel in one step. Pricing is public and your program price covers everything in it.
